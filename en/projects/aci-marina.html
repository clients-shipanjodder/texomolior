ACI marina

  • Name of the project

    ACI marina

  • Project Details

    COMMENCEMENT DATE: November 2021.
    COMPLETION DATE: June 2022.

  • Location

    Dubrovnik

Project details

Two projects Reconstruction of the Coastal Wall GAT I and Reconstruction of Infrastructure

1. GAT I - A completely new waterfront based on piles of a total of 82 piles with a diameter of fi 800 with a depth of 12 m in clay soil was built on this project. The pilots were connected by 41 beams on the main beams, and 82 beams supported them. The total width of the waterfront is 6.5 m, and the length is 232.31 m. These works enabled the mooring of ships larger than 20 m. A new hydraulic and sanitary network and electrical installations were built on the said waterfront.

2. Infrastructure - this project includes the reconstruction of the substation and electrical installation and the installation of yachts cabinets of different strength to moor ships up to 55m throughout the marina, water supply and hydrant networks in the length of 5km and sewerage 350m. The marina has 20 km of power cables and 7 km of optical cables. reconstruction of public lighting in the entire marina was performed. As final layers, asphalt was made in the amount of 2000m2 and 1500m2
