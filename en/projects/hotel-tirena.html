Hotel Tirena

  • Construction duration

    Completion date: June 2023.

  • Location

    Dubrovnik

Project details

Hotel Tirena is an existing building with a complex floor plan. Situated on terrain that descends in steps, three floors directly interact with the land: the ground floor serves as the entrance level, basement -1 as the restaurant, and basement -2 lies in a further descendingpart of the terrain. The primary entrance is positioned on the south side, while a commercialentrance is on the east side. The building completely integrates into the surrounding environment, with vegetation close to the glass walls. Hallways connecting the rooms are largely one-sided, ensuring natural light. The hotel has single and double roofs covered withroofing tiles. Currently, Hotel Tirena holds a three-star classification.

The renovation project for Hotel Tirena encompasses several levels of intervention, including:

  • General arrangement of all guest accommodations and public spaces within the hotel (such as rooms, lobby, hallways, restaurant), involving the replacement of outdated installations.
  • Adaptation of the restaurant, kitchen, and dining areas to meet new regulatory standards (such as HACCP) and the requirements for a higher hotel classification.
  • Replacement of existing old elevators and the addition of a new elevator within the building.
  • Replacement of the deteriorated entrance canopy with a new canopy designed to meet classification standards and accommodate bus reception.

Additionally, the hotel entrance and all public areas will undergo remodeling, including the lobby, reception, administrative offices, and staff areas. Renovation efforts will extend to areas adjacent to the lobby, including the lobby bar, which will feature an outdoor bar area (neutral - without equipment).
