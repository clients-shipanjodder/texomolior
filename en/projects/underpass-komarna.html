Underpass Komarna

  • Name of the project

    Underpass Komarna

  • Construction duration

    Commencement date: July 2020.
    Completion date: April 2021.

  • Location

    Pelješac

Project details

The D8 underpass near Komarna was built as part of the construction of the access road leading to the Pelješac Bridge. Situated at the intersection of the access road and the D8 state road, this underpass spans a length of 25.60 meters with a total width of 12 meters. It comprises a roadway with a width of 9.0 meters and inspection walkways measuring 0.75 meters in width..Structurally, it is a reinforced concrete monolithic frame with a spanning plate and abutment walls of thickness d=1.0 meter. The construction process involved on-site assembly using a fixed scaffolding system. Safety measures include a protective barrier and a pedestrian fence with an additional net to guard against thrown objects.
