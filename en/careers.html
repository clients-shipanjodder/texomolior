Careers

Texo Molior pays a great importance and attention to the employees of various profiles. When choosing new members of our professional team, we strive for prefesionality, dedication to work, and appropriate competences for the appropriate workplace.

We currently employ more than 176 workers at various positions required for the functioning and operation of the company. Given the rapid expansion of the business, and the great demand for Texo Molior's services, the firm is constantly employing construction workers of all profiles.

Joining the Texo Molior team means deciding on constant professional training and career building, and working on top projects! With us, people make the difference!
